WHO WE ARE

We're an interdisciplinary student group with members from both Brown and RISD.

OUR MISSION is to create a collaborative space for students of all disciplines to learn about digital artmaking, to build cool projects, to exhibit their work, and to meet people who are passionate about the intersection of technology and the arts.
